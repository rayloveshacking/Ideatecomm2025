About Ageless Bicyclists

Singapore's first ASD cycling program

Founded in 2015, Ageless Bicyclists is a non-profit social enterprise pioneering experiential inclusive recreation cycling for individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder. We are proud members of:

raiSE - Singapore Centre for Social Enterprise
ARAIBA - ASEAN Responsible And Inclusive Business Alliance
Asean Autism Network - Associate Member
Brands For Good - Honouree Recognition
